Studio Viana is a digital art and wallpaper design studio focused on clarity, proportion, and timeless visual storytelling. Founded by Vicky Rana, the studio creates custom mural wallpapers, 3D visualizations, and brand identity systems.

Every design starts with the space — its light, scale, and mood. Whether it’s a quiet bedroom needing serene depth, a nursery ready to ignite imagination, or a boutique entrance demanding presence, each piece is custom-crafted for that exact wall.

Studio Viana work is available on Adobe Stock, through direct commission, and as part of our growing printed wallpaper collection shipped across India.

Principles

  • Material honesty
  • Quiet, confident systems
